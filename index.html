Coconut Oil: A Versatile Beauty Product

By: Arnetta Knight

Updated | July 03, 2020



Coconut Oil isn’t just for cooking. Did you know that you can also swap out some of your beauty products in place of it! I’ve already made the switch, and I can officially call myself a coconut enthusiast. You can use coconut oil to hydrate & moisturize the skin, for hair care, and as a makeup remover. I also use it in my health and weight-loss efforts. Below is a list of its many uses!

  1. Hair Conditioner
  2. Body oil
  3. Lip Balm
  4. Under Eye Cream
  5. Frizz Control
  6. Make-up Remover
  7. Body Scrub
  8. Shaving Cream
